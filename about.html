Ngasa Raphael


I am a UI/UX Designer and Full Stack Developer based in Germany. My journey into Web Dev kicked off in 2018 during my days as an Intern in Paris as a compulsory module for my Masters studies in Sociology. I hold of a Bachelors Degree in Business Law, a Masters Degree in International Development and Cooperation, and a second Masters of Arts degree in Sociology.




"User empathy is key to all my designs"

Outside of coding, I find contentment in the active provision of aid designed to alleviate suffering and add value to the lifes of those in need. It is for these reasons that I love engaging in humanitarian activities and topics related to the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals.
