Handy Service

Find top rated handy men in your city


Overview

Designed to bridge the gap between handymen and clients, Handy-services provides a platform where home and office owners can find top rated handymen in their cities. After understanding the “one-time-service” nature of handy jobs, I was motivated to design an app where handy experts could easily be contacted for a quick service. By registering an account, users have access to five main handy categories namely; Carpenter, Plumber, Electrician, Painter and Tile setter.

Role

Covered all UX/UI Design methodologies employed in this project from research to prototyping

Problem

With demands for Handy services steadily increasing, the need to create an online presence has become unavoidable

Hypothesis

Building a solid online presence will increase a handyman’s client base, market security and control over his business.

Solution

Handy Service (the app) is designed to bridge the gap between Handymen and clients by giving the former better online presence

Usability test

With the need to create a user-centered design, I commenced my usability test by getting user stories after providing them with a synopsis of what Handy Service is all about. Using the prototype I made with Figma, the next step was querilla testing intended to further reveal the users emotions and impressions about the app.

Feedback

Out of five participants of the querilla test, three strongly agreed that the app was very easy to use while the other two had a neutral opinion. Nevertheless, users feedback promted the following updates in the final design


  • Add job option : While testing did not involve going through uploading a job on the app, options of users being able to add jobs was expected by a couple of users. This was not present in the initial designs
  • Hourly rates : One user expected hourly rates to be on expert's profile, an option worth considering but was left for negotiation between client and handyman
  • White spacing: Some initial designs were apparently sophisticated with unnecessary icons and animations
